# Day 4: Statistical Outlier Detection

## 🏢 The Business Scenario
A ride-sharing startup is auditing their driver payouts. [cite_start]Some drivers have figured out a glitch in the app to charge artificially high fares. 

## 🎯 The Objective
1. [cite_start]Calculate the "Price per Mile" for every single ride[cite: 238].
2. [cite_start]Use descriptive statistics (specifically, the Interquartile Range or IQR) to mathematically identify and isolate the rides that are statistically anomalous.

* [cite_start]`Distance_Miles`: Total distance of the trip[cite: 237].
